More About Me & What I Do

Hi and thank you for visiting me on my little corner of the internet. I'm currently a Computer Science PhD student at the University of Central Florida in Orlando, FL where my advisor is Dr. Carolina Cruz-Neira. I'm also a graduate research assistant at the VARLab@UCF headed by Dr. Cruz-Neira and Dr. Dirk Reiners.

My goal as a PhD student and a researcher is to be both a scientist and an artist. As more creatives move into VR as a medium for their art, they will need the technical expertise of VR experts. My hope is to push both VR as an form of expression and a vehicle for compelling narratives while also pushing the boundaires of what the technology is capable of.
